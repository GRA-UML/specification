Prescription Drug Monitoring Information Exchange IEPD

The PMIX service provides state PMP systems with the capability to retrieve interstate prescription drug history to assist in the identification of potential abuse and diversion. The service can provide the requested drug history as a direct response to a request containing person identifiers. In some cases, a delayed response will be provided. This will require the hosting of an associated service for delivery of the deferred response.
